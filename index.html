ABC School

ABC school is located in Gottigerre in Bangalore. It spreads over an area of 1000 acres. There are separate buildings for Pre-primary,primary, middle school,secondary and senior secondary wings. The school is there for more than 3 decades. The school is run by efficient management and has well qualified staff members. The school is affiliated to CBSE and ISO certified.

Library

There are 2 Libraries - one for the primary wing and one for the senior wing. There are more than 10,000 subject and story books across various journals. The Library in the school is a store house of knowledge. Children from Classes IV and above can borrow library books to read at home.

Science Laboratory

There are separate Physics, Chemistry and Biology laboratories to perform experiments in each subject for Classes VIII to XII. The Laboratories have advanced equipments and facilities to conduct experiments.

Computer Laboratory

There are 3 Computer laboratories and one Robotics Laboratory. The labs have all together 200 laptops with Windows Operating system and advanced softwares for children to work and have hands-on experience.

Playground

The school has a big playground where children play various sports. The school sports day is conducted in the playground every year. Various activities like yoga also happen in the playground. Morning assemblies are conducted in the school amphi theatre and playground.
